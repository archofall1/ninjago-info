The Story

Following the fall of Ninjago City, the team is split across two worlds. Lloyd and Nya hide in the shadows of the city, leading a small resistance of Elemental Masters against Emperor Garmadon’s iron-fisted rule. Lloyd must find a way to lead without his powers, proving that he is more than just his element.

Meanwhile, the original four Ninja and a young, rapidly-growing Wu are stranded in the Realm of Oni and Dragons. They are hunted by the Iron Baron and his brutal gang of Dragon Hunters. To get home, they must survive the 'Barren Wasteland' and earn the trust of the Firstbourne—the mother of all dragons. The season culminates in a dual battle: Lloyd facing his father in a test of spirit, and the Ninja returning on the backs of dragons to take back their home from the Colossus.

The Story

With the Great Devourer defeated but Lord Garmadon now in possession of the Four Golden Weapons, the Ninja begin training Lloyd for the ultimate confrontation. Garmadon uses his Mega Weapon to create havoc, including bringing a group of prehistoric pirates to life and creating 'Evil Ninja' clones. Meanwhile, the Ninja discover the mystery of the Dark Island, where the source of all evil—the Overlord—is waiting.

As Lloyd undergoes a rapid aging process to reach his full power, the team travels to the Dark Island to strike at the heart of darkness. There, Garmadon builds the Garmatron, a weapon of pure corruption. In a tragic twist, the Overlord possesses Garmadon’s body, transforming into a massive shadow dragon. Lloyd must reach deep within himself to unlock the forbidden Golden Power, transforming into the Ultimate Spinjitzu Master to light up the sky and banish the darkness forever.

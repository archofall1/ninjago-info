Biography & Lore

Zane is a Nindroid (Ninja Droid) created by Dr. Julien to protect those who cannot protect themselves. For a long time, Zane did not know he was a robot, believing he was just an orphan with strange habits.

His "funny bone" and logical perspective make him the brains of the group. He famously sacrificed himself to defeat the Overlord, later being rebuilt as the Titanium Ninja.

The Story

A new threat emerges from the past: Krux and Acronix, the Masters of Time who were defeated by Wu and Garmadon decades ago. Known as the 'Hands of Time,' they seek to collect the four Time Blades—artifacts that control the flow of time itself. They build a massive army of Vermillion soldiers made of millions of tiny snakes, capable of regenerating instantly from any wound.

As Master Wu begins to age rapidly due to a 'Time Punch,' Kai and Nya discover the shocking truth that their parents might have been traitors who helped the Time Twins. The search for the truth leads them to the Iron Doom, a giant time-traveling mech. In a climactic battle across the stream of time, the Ninja manage to save the past and future, but Master Wu stays behind on the Iron Doom to ensure the Time Twins are lost forever, leaving the Ninja without their mentor.

Biography & Lore

Harumi was the adopted Princess of Ninjago, but secretly she harbored a deep hatred for the Ninja. She blamed them for the loss of her parents during the Great Devourer's attack.

Operating as "The Quiet One," she orchestrated the resurrection of Lord Garmadon’s darkest form. Her relationship with Lloyd is one of the most tragic stories in Ninjago history, as she constantly wavers between her desire for revenge and her lingering feelings for him.

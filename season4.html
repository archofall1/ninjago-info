Tournament of Elements

← Back to Main Page
Main Villain: Master Chen
Key Event: Discovery of the Elemental Masters
The Goal: Finding Zane (The Titanium Ninja)

The Story

Mourning the loss of Zane, the Ninja team has drifted apart, each taking on odd jobs to fill the void. Their lives are upended when they receive a mysterious invitation to Master Chen’s Tournament of Elements, with a cryptic hint that Zane might still be alive. They travel to a remote, private island where they encounter other Elemental Masters—users of Shadow, Amber, Gravity, Mind, and more—all competing for glory.

They soon discover the tournament is a front for a dark ritual. Chen is using his 'Staff of Elements' to steal the powers of every loser to cast a spell that will transform him and his cult into Anacondrai warriors. The Ninja must unite the rival Elemental Masters into a single army. The war spills into the Corridor of Elders, where Garmadon makes the ultimate sacrifice, banishing himself to the Cursed Realm to summon the spirits of the original Anacondrai Generals to stop Chen’s false army.

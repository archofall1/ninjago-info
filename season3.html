The Story

Years after the Final Battle, Ninjago has transformed into a high-tech metropolis called New Ninjago City, built by the genius Cyrus Borg. However, peace is shattered when the Overlord returns as a digital virus, seizing control of Borg’s security systems and an army of Nindroids. The Ninja are stripped of their elemental powers and given Techno-Blades—the only tools capable of hacking the Overlord's digital reign.

The conflict scales from the digital 'Digiverse' all the way into outer space, as the Nindroids attempt to recover the Golden Weapons from the stars to help the Overlord become the 'Golden Master.' In the heartbreaking finale, the Golden Master begins a path of total destruction through the city. Zane, realizing he is the only one who can stop the monster, uses his heart's power source to overload the Overlord’s energy, sacrificing his physical body to save his friends and the world he was built to protect.

Biography & Lore

Kalmaar was the prince of the Merlopian kingdom who held a deep hatred for surface dwellers. He betrayed his own father, King Trimaar, to take the throne and wake the storm spirit, Wojira.

He sought to use Wojira to flood Ninjago and wipe out the surface world. His arrogance eventually led to his downfall when he lost control of the beast he had awoken.

The Story

The peace is shattered when Lloyd is suddenly possessed by the vengeful ghost of Morro, the Master of Wind and Wu’s very first student. Morro believes he was cheated out of being the Green Ninja and is determined to claim the Realm Crystal to bring his master, The Preeminent, into Ninjago. Without Lloyd’s power, the remaining Ninja are nearly helpless against Morro’s wind attacks and ghostly allies.

To fight back, the Ninja must travel to the Cloud Kingdom and the Tomb of the First Spinjitzu Master, learning the lost art of Airjitzu along the way. Simultaneously, Nya discovers a secret her mother kept from her: she is the Master of Water. As the Preeminent—a living realm of cursed souls—begins to manifest in the village of Stiix, Nya must overcome her fear of failure to unlock her true power. The season ends with a watery showdown where the Ninja must sink an entire city to drown the ghostly threat.

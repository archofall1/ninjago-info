The Story

After the Pilot episodes, the Ninja face a new threat: the Serpentine. Lloyd Garmadon accidentally releases the snake tribes, leading to Pythor uniting them to summon the Great Devourer.

This season is famous for the Ninja unlocking their "True Potential" and the massive reveal of who the Green Ninja actually is.

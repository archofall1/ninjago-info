Biography & Lore

The Overlord is the ancient source of all evil in Ninjago, the dark counterpart to the First Spinjitzu Master. He cannot be truly destroyed as long as there is darkness in the world.

Whether appearing as a giant dragon, a digital virus, or the Golden Master, his goal remains the same: to tip the balance of Ninjago into eternal darkness and rule over the ruins of the world.

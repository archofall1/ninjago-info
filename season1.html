The Story

After being banished from Darkley's School for Bad Boys, a young and bratty Lloyd Garmadon seeks to follow in his father's villainous footsteps. He accidentally stumbles upon the ancient tomb of the Hypnobrai, kickstarting a chain reaction that releases all five Serpentine tribes upon Ninjago. As the snakes unite under the cunning and treacherous Pythor P. Chumsworth, the Ninja must race to find the four Silver Fangblades.

Throughout this journey, Kai, Jay, Cole, and Zane each face personal trials to unlock their 'True Potential.' The stakes reach a fever pitch when it is revealed that the destiny of the Green Ninja belongs not to Kai, as he had hoped, but to the very boy who started the mess: Lloyd. The season culminates in a massive battle against the Great Devourer, a snake so large it threatens to consume the entire world, forcing an unlikely alliance between the Ninja and Lord Garmadon.

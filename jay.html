Biography & Lore

Jay was raised in a junkyard by his adoptive parents, Ed and Edna. This background gave him a natural talent for inventing and mechanics, often building gadgets out of scrap metal.

He is the first ninja to ever perform Spinjitzu. Despite his frequent panicking and jokes, Jay is incredibly brave when it comes to protecting Nya, the love of his life.
